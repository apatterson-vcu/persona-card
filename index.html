Alex Chen

"The Skeptical Shopper"

Casual Gamer

Alex is a 20-year-old who games casually on weekends between part-time work and classes. With a tight budget, every gaming purchase feels risky because they can't afford to buy the wrong equipment.

Goals

  • Find reliable, honest reviews from real customers who actually bought the product
  • Understand product specifications in plain language without technical jargon
  • Get genuine deals without falling for fake urgency tactics or inflated "discounts"

Frustrations

  • Untrustworthy Reviews: "My keyboard died. I delayed buying because I didn't trust reviews."
  • Confusing Information: "Specs not matching across sites. One page says it works on Mac, another says Windows only."
  • Cluttered Design: "Auto-playing videos and cluttered pages. Pop-ups, fake urgency timers everywhere."

Based on user research for gaming equipment shopping experience
